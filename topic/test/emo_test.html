Example

You can either refer to emoji by their name (which is unique):

emo::ji("poop")
#> <U+0001F4A9>

Or by a keyword. Keywords are not unique so emo::ji will pick one for you at random.

emo::ji("face")
#> <U+0001F479>
emo::ji("face")
#> <U+0001F42E>
emo::ji("face")
#> <U+0001F61A>

Often you’ll use inline like `r emo::ji("smile")` 😄
